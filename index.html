Core gameplay

Play to Earn

In Chain Rivals, users can play a blockchain-powered game that allows them to earn the project's native token, $RIVALS. The game features various challenges and tournaments that reward players with $RIVALS for their achievements. As players progress through the game and build their skills, they can accumulate more $RIVALS, which they can then use to participate in the game's economy, such as buying in-game items or staking their tokens to earn additional rewards. Overall, Chain Rivals provides an engaging and rewarding gaming experience for players while also fostering a thriving ecosystem around the $RIVALS token.

Wager System

In Chain Rivals, users can wager their $RIVALS tokens in multiplayer matches to earn even more tokens. The game's matchmaking system pairs users with similar skill levels, and players can place bets on the outcome of the match using $RIVALS. The winner of the match earns the pot, which includes the wagers of both players, in addition to a bonus payout in $RIVALS. This feature adds an extra layer of excitement to the gameplay and allows players to earn more tokens while competing against other users. It also helps to increase the overall demand and liquidity for the $RIVALS token.

NFT Utility

Chain Rivals also features NFTs that seamlessly integrate into the game ecosystem. These NFTs represent unique digital assets that players can collect, trade, and use in the game. Each NFT has different attributes and characteristics that provide various benefits to the player, such as access to exclusive game modes, additional resources, or new abilities. By using NFTs, players can customize their gaming experience and access additional content that is not available to non-NFT holders. Additionally, the ownership and scarcity of these digital assets create a vibrant marketplace around them, allowing users to trade them and potentially earn even more $RIVALS.

Chain rivals road map

Phase 1: Initial Development (Q1 2023 - Q2 2023)

-

In the initial phase, the focus will be on developing the core gameplay mechanics and infrastructure for Chain Rivals.

-

The team will work on building the game engine, designing the user interface, and implementing the blockchain infrastructure for $RIVALS.

-

The team will also begin creating the first set of NFTs that will be integrated into the game.

Phase 2: Alpha Testing and Balancing (Q3 2023 - Q4 2023)

-

In the second phase, the game will move into alpha testing to gather feedback from a small group of users.

-

The team will use the feedback to fine-tune the game mechanics, balance the economy, and optimize the user experience.

-

The team will also work on building the multiplayer matchmaking system and integrating the wagering mechanics.

Phase 3: Public Beta and Marketing (Q1 2024 - Q2 2024)

-

In the third phase, Chain Rivals will enter into a public beta, allowing a larger audience to play and test the game.

-

The team will launch a marketing campaign to attract users to the platform and generate awareness for $RIVALS.

-

The team will also continue to create and release new NFTs that will unlock new features and content for the game.

Phase 4: Full Launch and Ecosystem Expansion (Q3 2024 - Q4 2024)

-

In the final phase, Chain Rivals will launch fully, with all gameplay mechanics and features in place.

-

The team will continue to release new NFTs, events, and challenges to keep the community engaged and the game fresh.

-

The team will also work on expanding the ecosystem around $RIVALS by integrating with other blockchain projects and gaming platforms, making it easier for users to acquire and use $RIVALS outside of Chain Rivals.
